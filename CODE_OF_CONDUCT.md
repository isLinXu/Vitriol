## Code Of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and patience
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Respecting different viewpoints and experience levels
- Focusing criticism on ideas, code, and process rather than people
- Helping keep discussions technical, clear, and welcoming

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or demeaning language
- Trolling, inflammatory comments, or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Repeatedly ignoring reasonable maintainer guidance

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- Issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Project chat spaces, if any
- Any other public spaces associated with the project

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation for repeated or serious violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers through the repository's security/contact channel.

When reporting, include:

- What happened
- Links or screenshots if relevant
- Approximate time and context
- Any immediate safety concerns

All good-faith reports will be reviewed and handled as confidentially as practical.
